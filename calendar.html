    January

    • Winter Carnival -
      2024-01-01

      A festive event celebrating the joys of winter with activities such as ice skating, snow sculpting contests, and sleigh rides. This is a popular event in Nocturne Falls, drawing visitors from both near and far.

    • Polar Plunge -
      2024-01-20

      A charity event where participants brave the cold by taking a dip in icy waters, often to raise funds for a worthy cause. In Nocturne Falls, this event takes on a unique twist as plungers have the chance to swim with mermaids, and don't forget to keep an eye out for the mysterious leviathan swimming in the lakes depths.

    February

    • Valentine's Day Bake Off -
      2024-02-14

      Indulge your senses and ignite your passion for baking in our delightful celebration of love and culinary creativity, where we come together to showcase their most delectable treats. Whether you're a seasoned pastry chef or a budding home baker, this is your chance to shine! Craft your most enchanting confections infused with love and magic, and compete for the title of Bake Off Champion.

    • Cupid's Ball -
      2024-02-24

      A romantic affair held in a grand venue, featuring elegant decorations, live music, and dancing under the moonlit sky. Supernatural beings and humans alike enjoy a magical night whether you are seeking love or simply want to enjoy the festive atmosphere.

      2024-03-09

      As spring approaches, Nocturne Falls residents gather to learn about magical gardening techniques and cultivate your own enchanted gardens. This workshop includes tips on growing mystical plants, creating fairy habitats, and attracting beneficial supernatural creatures to the garden.

    • Leprechaun Treasure Hunt -
      2024-03-17

      A fun and adventurous activity organized for children and adults alike, where participants search for hidden treasures scattered throughout the town. In Nocturne Falls, these treasures could be guarded by mischievous leprechauns, requiring wit and cunning to uncover.
